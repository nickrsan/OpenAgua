The next-generation decision support system for water resources planning.  The goal of this project is to offer the opportunity to share and learn through open source collaboration between researchers, politicians, stakeholders and students.


Features of OpenAgua:

Optimization water modeling

OpenAgua uses optimization-based simulation modeling of water resources systems.

Sharing for collaboration 

Easy and flexible sharing for collaboration between experts, decision-makers, and stakeholders.

Standardized data organization

Standardized data organization is  based on Hydra Platform

Advanced scenario builder 

Advanced scenario builder to rapidly explore a wide range of management alternatives and social, economic and environmental scenarios.

Cloud computing

Flexible computing options, leveraging the power of cloud computing.

Advanced visualization tools

Advanced visualization tools, including popular third-party graphing options (e.g., Tableau and Plotly)

Open source

Open source, with a BSD license. Feel free to fork from GitHub and help improve OpenAgua, or host from your own computer.

Security

Secure interphase system
