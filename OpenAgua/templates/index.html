Welcome to OpenAgua!

OpenAgua is a next-generation decision support system for water resources planning.

Planned features include:

  • Optimization-based simulation modeling of water resources systems. (learn more)
  • Standardized data organization based on Hydra Platform. (learn more)
  • Easy and flexible sharing for collaboration between experts, decision-makers, and stakeholders. (learn more)
  • Advanced scenario builder to rapidly explore a wide range of management alternatives and social, economic and environmental scenarios. (learn more)
  • Flexible computing options, leveraging the power of cloud computing. (learn more)
  • Advanced visualization tools, including popular third-party graphing options (e.g., Tableau and Plotly). (learn more)
  • Open source, with a BSD license. Feel free to fork from GitHub and help improve OpenAgua, or host from your own computer. (learn more)
  • Secure everything. (learn more)
